# Overview: Who Is Christ? --- ## Where We Are In Defenders * We’ve covered: * Doctrine of revelation (general and special) * Doctrine of God (attributes, natural theology) * Doctrine of the Trinity * Doctrine of the Holy Spirit * Now: **Christology** — the doctrine of Christ --- ## Christology: Person and Work * Two questions structure the doctrine of Christ * **Person:** Who is Jesus Christ? * **Work:** What did Jesus do? * In Defenders, this tracks four major topics: * **Incarnation** (deity + humanity in one person) * **Death** (atonement) * **Resurrection** (vindication and historical case) * **Access** (who can be saved through Christ) --- ## Why This Matters * The incarnation and atonement are the heart of distinctively Christian theism * Not merely “generic monotheism” * The doctrine of Christ anchors: * Worship (who we worship) * The gospel (what God has done) * Assurance and hope (resurrection) * Evangelism and missions (access) --- ## Terms We’ll Use * **Incarnation:** the Son assumes a human nature * **Atonement:** what Christ achieves by his death for sinners * **Resurrection:** God raises Jesus bodily from the dead * **Particularism:** salvation is through Christ (not religious pluralism) --- ## Navigation * [Return to Christ Overview](Christ.Slides.html) * Next: [The Incarnation: Two Natures, One Person](Christ.Slides.2.IncarnationTwoNaturesOnePerson.html) * [Return to Creekside Defenders](../CreeksideDefenders.html) ---
