# Overview: The Holy Spirit in Christian Doctrine --- ## Where We Are In Defenders * Recently: The Doctrine of God (existence and nature of God) * Attributes of God * Natural theology and objections * Doctrine of the Trinity * Next: **Pneumatology** — the doctrine of the Holy Spirit * The Holy Spirit is often called “the forgotten person of the Trinity” --- ## Pneumatology * From Greek *pneuma* (“spirit”, “wind”) * Personhood: Who is the Spirit? - A person * Deity: Is the Spirit God? - Yes * Work: What does the Spirit do? - creation, revelation, regeneration, sanctification, empowerment, ... * Goal: see the Spirit’s role across Scripture and Christian life --- ## “Forgotten Person” (Why?) * Early church controversies focused on Christology and Trinity * Limited early reflection on the Spirit * Even creeds can sound brief about the Spirit * e.g., “and in the Holy Spirit” * Modern neglect can show up in theology and practice * “God, man, and Christ” as a curriculum title is telling * Goal: recover both the **person** and **work** of the Spirit --- ## Navigation * [Return to Holy Spirit Overview](HolySpirit.Slides.html) * Next: [Personhood of the Holy Spirit](HolySpirit.Slides.2.html) * [Creekside Defenders Main Page](http://tinyurl.com/CreeksideDefenders) ---
