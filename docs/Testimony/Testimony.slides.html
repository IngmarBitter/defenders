**Christian Testimony** How to make it an effective evangelism tool
Ingmar Bitter --- ## Why !!! ”Always be ready to give a defence to anyone who asks you for a reason for the hope that is in you. However, do this with gentleness and respect” 1 Peter 3:15 HCSB ![](hope.jpg style="float: right; width: 8rem") * Sharing your testimony is the shortes form of putting 1 Peter 3:15 into practice * It communicates + your hope + your reasons for your hope * Let's get ready! --- ## When ![](coffeeTalk.jpg style="float:right; width: 9rem") * Anytime someone asks * Anytime someone is looking for hope --- ## What ![](threeCircles.jpg style="float:right; width: 9rem") 1. Your life before Jesus 2. How you got to believe in Jesus + clear explanation of the gospel 3. Your life with Jesus + maturing and joy You are the world's foremost expert of your own history! --- ## How ![](readingScroll.jpg style="float:right; width: 9rem") * Write out you testimony on one page * Read it in one to three minutes * Read it to us for constructive criticism * One testimony each week * Homework: write your testimony story * [**Example**](TestimonyIngmar.html) ---
